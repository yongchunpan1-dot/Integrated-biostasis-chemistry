# Active Library Expansion Plan

## Goal

Expand the IBC active library from ~60 materials to 200-500 high-confidence preservation-relevant materials.

## Inclusion Criteria

- Literature support
- Biocompatibility or regulatory precedent
- Plausible preservation mechanism
- Commercial availability
- Aqueous formulation compatibility

## Source Domains

1. Cryopreservation
2. Lyophilization
3. Vaccine stabilization
4. Protein formulation
5. Extracellular vesicle preservation
6. Cell therapy formulation
7. Biomaterials
8. FDA IID excipients
9. GRAS ingredients
10. USP/NF excipients

## Mechanism Categories

### Structural Stabilization
Target: 100+ materials

### Interface Stabilization
Target: 50+ materials

### Chemical Stabilization
Target: 150+ materials

### Physical Encapsulation
Target: 100+ materials

## Long-Term Vision

Active Library: 200-500 materials
Candidate Library: 500-2000 materials
Universe Library: >10000 materials

The active library is used for formulation generation. Larger libraries serve as future searchable chemical spaces.
